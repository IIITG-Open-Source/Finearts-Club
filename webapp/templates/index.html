About Us

Alankrit means to decorate, and that is what art does to our otherwise mundane life. Art is the way we express ourselves and seek to understand what lies within, and that is what, Alankrit - the Fine arts club of IIITG aspires to be. We at Alankrit wish to create a community of talented artists who add wings to their imagination and color life in all its glory.
